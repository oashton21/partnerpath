Founded in 1960, YSB is one of the largest and most comprehensive non-profit agencies serving youth in the Ottawa community, with more than 350 professionals working from 20 locations across the city. YSB provides services in employment, health and housing, mental health, youth engagement, and youth justice, in both English and French, working in partnership to develop, deliver, and advocate for better conditions and opportunities for youth and families.
